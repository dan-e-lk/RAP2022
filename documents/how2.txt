Before going out to the field.
1. check the battery level of your tablet
2. make sure you have Terraflex app and make sure you can log in.
3. on your Terraflex app, make sure you can open RAP project.
4. prepare a map + GPS or a geoPDF map pinpointing where each of the clusters are located.


When you are in the field.
1. Make sure your GPS is on.
2. When you arrive at the project location, open Terraflex - RAP project and fill out the project survey form.
